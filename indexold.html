Who we are

SRM Team Robocon is the Robotics Team of SRM University, Chennai. We are involved in competitive robotics and also research and development in various aspects related to the field of robotics.

We take part in the annually organized ABU Robocon – Asia’s biggest robotics contest which aims to promote friendship among like-minded youths who will be future leaders, as well as help advance engineering technologies.

SRM Team Robocon is a multidisciplinary team comprising individuals from different branches and different years but with one thing in common – a passion for technology and innovation. The participating engineers are ingenious and equipped with a diverse skill set and the technical, practical and management knowhow required to quench their thirst for innovation.
